🎸 吉他指板学习指南

GuitarFB 是一款免费在线吉他指板工具，帮助吉他手快速掌握指板上的音阶与和弦指法。无论你是刚入门还是进阶演奏者，这套工具都能辅助你的日常练习。

音阶与调性

首先在"调性"下拉菜单中选择您想要练习的根音（如 C、G、A），然后在"音阶"列表中选择一种调式——从自然大调、自然小调到布鲁斯音阶、日本音阶等共 14 种。点击✨ 音阶按钮，指板上会以彩色标注突出显示该调性下的所有音符位置。

和弦构成

选择根音和调式后，在"级数"下拉菜单中指定和弦级数（I 级到 VII 级），再选择和弦类型——三和弦、七和弦、九和弦等。点击🎼 和弦按钮生成对应的和弦指法图。您还可以切换音名 / 唱名 / 音程三种标注模式，帮助理解每个音符在和弦中的角色。

练习技巧

循环练习：选择一个调性的所有级数和弦，逐个生成并在指板上找出共同音与变化音。
调弦自定义：每块指板左下角的调音按钮可独立设置弦音，支持 Drop D、Open G 等特殊调弦。
多指板对比：点击右上角"＋"按钮添加新指板，可同时对比不同调性或调式的指板布局。
标注系统：使用手动标注和音高标注在指板上标记重点位置。

节拍器辅助

内置节拍器支持 BPM 调节、拍号选择、节奏细分（八分/十六分/三连音）、重音切换和定时练习。点击 BPM 数字可直接输入数值，👆 按钮可手动打点测速。

🎸 Guitar Fretboard Learning Guide

GuitarFB is a free online guitar fretboard tool that helps guitarists quickly master scales and chord shapes on the fretboard. Whether you're a beginner or an advanced player, this tool supports your daily practice.

Scales & Keys

Select a root note from the Key dropdown (C, G, A, etc.), then pick a scale mode from the list — Major, Minor, Blues, Japanese, and 10 more. Click ✨ Scale to highlight every note of that scale on the fretboard with color-coded markers.

Chord Construction

After choosing a key and scale, pick a chord degree (I through VII) and a chord type — Triad, 7th, 9th, 11th, or 13th. Click 🎼 Chord to generate the chord diagram. Switch between Note / Solfege / Interval modes to understand each note's role in the chord.

Practice Tips

Cycle Practice: Generate all degrees of a key and find common tones vs. changing notes.
Custom Tuning: Each fretboard has its own tuning button (bottom-left). Supports Drop D, Open G, DADGAD, and more.
Multi-Board Comparison: Click the "+" button to add fretboards and compare different keys or tunings side by side.
Annotations: Use manual markers and pitch labels to mark important positions.

Metronome

The built-in metronome supports BPM adjustment, time signature, rhythm subdivision (8th/16th/triplet), accent toggle, and countdown timer. Double-click the BPM value to type a number, or tap the 👆 button to tap tempo.
